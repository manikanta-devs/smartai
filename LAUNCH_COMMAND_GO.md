## 🎯 IF SOMETHING GOES TERRIBLE

**If you get 0 signups Day 1:**
- Don't panic. Not everyone sees launches immediately.
- Check: ProductHunt is it live? Twitter posted? Reddit posted?
- If not posted, POST NOW.
- If posted but no clicks, messaging needs work. Update post tomorrow.

**If you get bugs/crashes Day 1:**
- This is normal for any launch.
- Does site work? YES - keep going.
- Does site work? NO - fix it, redeploy, post update "Fixed!"

**If paying users don't convert:**
- Premium features unclear.
- Update copy: Be more specific about what premium gives you.
- Test different messages.
- This is NORMAL. Takes 2-3 weeks for conversion to stabilize.

**If support volume is overwhelming:**
- This is GOOD. Means users care.
- Auto-response email: "Replying in 24 hours"
- Handle them in batches (morning, evening)
- By week 2, hire VA to help if needed.

## 🎯 YOUR NORTH STAR METRICS

**Track these obsessively:**

1. **Daily Active Users** - People using your product today
2. **Free-to-Paid Conversion** - % of free users who upgrade
3. **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** - How much users recommend you (ask: "Rate 0-10 how likely to recommend")
4. **Churn** - % of users who stop using you
5. **Revenue** - Money collected today

**Rule of thumb targets:**
- Daily Active Users: 10%+ of signups (if you get 1K users, 100+ should use it daily)
- Free-to-Paid: 2-5% (out of 1K free users, 20-50 upgrade to paid)
- NPS: >50 is great (means people love it)
- Churn: <1% monthly (people stick around)
- Revenue: Compounds monthly (grows 20-50%+/month early)
